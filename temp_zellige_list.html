Classical, Contemporary and traditional Moroccan zellige in France

The zellige, zellij, zillij, zillige, الزليج(alzzalij), in its contemporary, traditional or classical version is a pure Moroccan craft product with essentially geometric design used to protect floors and walls until ground level also called Moroccan mosaic.
The zellige is in fact a terra cotta tile of about 5x5cm to 10x10cm depending of the time, decorative and cut with hammers by zellige craftsmen called the Maalems.
Our creative workshop is specialised in hand crafted production of more than 60 colors classical, contemporary or also traditional in various forms and design propesed for sale in dimensions 5x5cm or 10x10cm for the walling tiles and in 20x20cm for the flooring laying.
Zellige Maroc is the first online importer and exporter of Moroccan zellige and cement tiles thanks to important quality control of the production chain with moroccan craftsmen dedicating their knowledge to develop a large range of products collections from classical to contemporary but also traditional.
Zellige Maroc invites you to visit our collections of zelliges in its classical, contemporary an traditional version perfect for fountain projects, floors covering, walling but also table tops and other furnitures creations.
